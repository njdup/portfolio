About Me

I'm a junior at Stanford University studying computer science and working to make a name for myself in the everchanging tech scene of Silicon Valley.

I'm passionate about programming, hacking, and the impact technology can have on the world. Right now, my interests - which are ever-evolving - center around virtual reality, ed-tech, and gov-tech.

My short-term goals are focused on improving my abilities as a software engineer and hacker through working on exciting projects and challenging problems.
